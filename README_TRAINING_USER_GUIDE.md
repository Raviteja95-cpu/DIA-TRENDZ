## 📜 MODULE 2: INTERACTIVE OPERATIONAL PLAYBOOKS DESK
The secondary interface under training tabs contains high-fidelity text-based instruction cards. These playbooks can be selected by any manager or worker to find the exact compliance protocols for precious gold allocations.

### 📕 PLAYBOOK A: METALLIC LOSS ALLOCATION PROTOCOLS
* **Purpose:** Instructs workers and managers on how to balance workshops after filing precious metals.
* **Artisan Input:** Inputs finished gold weight *plus* saved scrap powder in the bench form.
* **System Formula Rule:**
  $$\text{Loss} = \text{Raw Dispatched Weight} - (\text{Finished weight} + \text{Collected shavings})$$
* **Security Threshold:** Limits deviations to $0.05 \text{ Grams}$. If deviation exceeds this threshold, a bright crimson warning alert blocks submission and registers a security report flags.

### 📘 PLAYBOOK B: CUSTODIAL TRANSFER PROCEDURES
* **Purpose:** Sets physical and digital handover standards.
* **Operational Steps:**
  1. The artisan confirms the unique barcode number of the high-value envelope.
  2. The artisan taps **"▶ Start Labor Work"** to take digital custody.
  3. The manager checks that status changes from "Assigned" to "In Progress". This shifts the gold asset responsibility immediately to that specific workstation employee's key.

## 📅 MODULE 6: THE DOUBLE-CUSTODY HOLIDAY PLANNER & METRIC CALCULATIONS

To coordinate vacations and ensure bullion is not allocated to absent artisans, the system runs a double-custody planner supporting full or half-day corporate dayoffs.

### 💼 HOW A CUSTOM HOLIDAY OR HALF DAY IS DECLARED:

#### 1. By the Team Lead / Admin (The Proposal Phase)
* **What it is:** Admins schedule or propose company closures.
* **Input Actions:**
  1. Open the **"Staff Attendance Planner"** tab.
  2. Input **Holiday Title / Occasion** (e.g., "Silver Jubilee Workshop Outing").
  3. Select **Duration Type**: Click **"Full Day Holiday"** or **"Half Day Holiday"**.
* **Date Syncing Logic for Half Day:**
  - Selecting **"Half Day Holiday"** automatically locks the **End Date** to align with the **Start Date** (as half-days are restricted to a single day). The End Date input field turns gray and displays `"End Date (Locked)"`.
  - The calculated duration is set at `0.5` days.
  - Adding Guidelines/Notice: Type "Morning shift active, workshop shuts down at 13:00".
  - Click **"Publish / Propose Holiday"**.
* **Expected Output:** The dayoff appears on the list as `PENDING OVERRIDE (0.5d)` with a yellow indicator. A live dashboard notification is routed to the Super Admin.

#### 2. By the Super Admin (The Absolute Approval & Override Phase)
* **What it is:** Only Super Admins authorize proposed dayoffs and finalize duration weights.
* **Input Actions & Clicks:**
  1. Login as Super Admin and navigate to the Holiday Planner.
  2. Find the pending holiday under **"Active Corporate Closure List"** containing `PENDING OVERRIDE`.
  3. **Super Admin Tool Box** (Input): Decide the final calculated days (defaults to `0.5` for half-days, or standard ranges for full holidays).
  4. Click **"Approve"** to authorize or **"Reject"** to discard.
* **Expected Output:** 
  - **Approve:** The status transitions to `APPROVED ({days} days)` and turns bright emerald green.
  - The calendar visual mockup updates: Approved dates are styled in rich gold with `"OUT"` indicators block.
  - Active artisan roster schedules update, automatically warning managers if assigning gold to absent workers.

#### 3. Personal Employee Leave Balances and absence lookups
* Employees can open their dashboard to view:
  - **Present Month Absence** (e.g. `2 days`).
  - **Past Months Absence Ledger** (e.g. `12 days`).
  - Personal vacation periods. Colleague vacation periods are automatically hidden to protect visual privacy, with an override button to toggle viewable schedules.
